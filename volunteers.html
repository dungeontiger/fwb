Volunteers
Fun with Books would not be successful without its volunteers. Volunteers prepare book packages and personally deliver them to the homes of our registered children six times each year. If you would like to volunteer, please contact Ann Kearney. We'd love to have you on board.
Fun With Books is a member of the Volunteer Bureau of Leeds and Grenville. You can volunteer, if you wish, through the Volunteer Bureau community hub.
