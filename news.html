Fun With Books is excited this month to announce the printing of Wendell the Wallaby. This is a special book as it was written and illustrated by the 2008 Grade 1 and 2 class of Heritage Community Christian School in New Dublin, Ontario. It recounts the touching true story of Wendell the Wallaby. The illustrations are bold and colorful being inspired by Canadian Artist Ted Harrison.

This month sees the release of Hannah's Little Red Wheelbarrow which was written by Emily Schaafsma and illustrated by Michelle Reid. It is a cheerful story of a little girl, Hannah, who helps her mother harvest vegetables from their garden, putting them into her wheelbarrow, counting as she goes.
Emily Schaafsma was born in Calgary, Albert and raised in Kingston, Ontario. She lives in Brockville where she is an elementary school teacher with the Upper Canada School Board.
Michelle Reid is a lifelong artist living and working in Kingston, Ontario.
